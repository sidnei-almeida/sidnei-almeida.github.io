Risk Score Methodology

The Investment Risk Score is calculated based on a weighted combination of economic indicators:

  • GDP: Higher GDP means lower risk (negative weight)
  • Inflation: Higher inflation means higher risk (positive weight)
  • Real Interest Rate: Very high rates indicate higher risk (positive weight)
  • Unemployment: High unemployment levels increase risk (positive weight)

Scale: 0-30 (Low Risk) | 30-60 (Moderate Risk) | 60-100 (High Risk)
